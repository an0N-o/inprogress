TECHNOLOGY

This Light Gauge steel system makes use of steel strength to weight ratio. Therefore by using high tensile steel we are able to achieve less thickness of steel for making it a structural member. A unique forming technique converts the steel into a more stable form of structural element which is designed based on the following :
1. DEAD LOAD
2. LIVE LOAD
3. SEISMIC LOAD
4. WIND LOAD
​

This entire structure
is much lighter
(almost 30-35 %) as compared to RCC building and is a highly earth quake resistant structure system

Most of the finishing materials used are factory finished high quality materials manufactured under controlled manner.

LGSF structure is CAD driven process and designed on dedicated software which provides complete output including drawings and Material BOQ.

Construction speed is much faster and it is easy to install without requiring heavy equipment such as cranes.

Our Light gauge Steel structures design process is a CAD based process using a dedicated and Intelligent detailing and design software, FRAMECAD STRUCTURE

FRAMECAD STRUCTURE, provided by the industry leader in Light Gauge Steel framing solutions. All the frames and sections are manufactured on FRAMECAD manufacturing equipment.
For more info visit : www.framecad.com

FEATURES, ADVANTAGE AND BENEFITS
  • Stability - We use Light Gauge Steel Frame (LGSF) which is ideal for high wind zone( 200 Kmph) as well as high seismic zone area (Zone V) as the dead load of entire structure system is much lower as compared to RCC.
  • Lighter & Stronger - This entire structure is much lighter (almost 30-35 %) but stronger as compared to RCC building and is a highly earth quake resistant structure system. The entire framing system is manufactured out of Hi-quality Hi tensile Galvanized steel.
  • Longer life and easy to maintain - It provides better habitable environment due to insulated constructions.
  • Building extensions - Being light weight this structure is suitable for all vertical expansions, even on older building structure.
  • Quality Structure - This system used high tensile steel (550Mpa) for structural members with anti-rust zinc coating of 275gsm making it completely weather proof.
  • Customized system - Complete LGSF system is designed as per the client requirements and comes with the design report proving the structural stability. This report can be verified by any competent authority.
  • Environment Friendly - All materials used for construction are factory finished material and are eco friendly in nature.
  • Compliance - This structure complies to all relevant Indian and international codes required for designing the system.
